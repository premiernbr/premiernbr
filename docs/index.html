About

The “Digital Minaret” hub groups together my LegalRAG experiments, structured open-data workflows, and educational content on justice, technology and access to clear legal information in Arabic and French.

I’m especially interested in tools that help court clerks and ordinary citizens obtain trustworthy answers in a language they actually understand, while respecting privacy, ethics and the limits of legal advice.

I work with a hybrid approach: local models (LM Studio, Ollama) plus cloud APIs when needed, combined with carefully curated Moroccan legal corpora and official open data.
