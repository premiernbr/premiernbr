About

I explore how AI and open data can support, not replace, humans — especially in the context of Moroccan justice and the everyday work of court clerks.

The “Digital Minaret” hub groups together my LegalRAG experiments for Moroccan users, structured open-data workflows, and educational content on justice, technology and access to clear legal information in Arabic and French.

Long term, the goal is simple: help people obtain trustworthy answers in a language they actually understand, while respecting privacy, ethics and the limits of legal advice.
